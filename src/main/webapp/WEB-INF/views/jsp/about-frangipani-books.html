Frangipani Books Ltd

Frangipani Books Ltd was established in 2001 and incorporated in 2006 as a producer of coffee table books and other specialized printing matter. Our niche in the market is relatively untapped and we are proud to have transacted business with The Office of the President of Ghana, The Olusegun Obasanjo Presidential Library Foundation, The Ghana Education Service and The Databank Group.

As a purveyor of fine printed material, we do not have a printing press operation. However, the arm of our business that deals in print brokering is preferred by many companies and as such has won several contracts to simply print matter for clients that have provided camera-ready copies of their books.

The quality of our books is unmatched on the Ghanaian and African publishing landscape and in recent times, we have been awarded contracts to print text- only books by clients impressed by our previous publications.

Frangipani retains on its staff the finest photographers, graphic designers, editors and print consultants. We buy print from all over the world and take our press check obligations very seriously to ensure that the books we publish for our clients are of the highest possible quality.
